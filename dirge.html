Dirge

We came
To bury
Our faith
Our precious pasts
Our tattered flags
The humbling
Hobbling
Torturous
Burden of
Dreams

Put them in the ground
Shoveled in the earth
No words
No sound
Just the leaves
On the wind
Trembling
And the hurt

From that seed
A mighty root
And it grew
